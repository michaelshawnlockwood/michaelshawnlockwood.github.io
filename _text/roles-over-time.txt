          <p>Here’s what the graphic shows: it maps my career (1997–present) as time-varying focus across four hats—Dev, 
            DBA, ETL, and Business-data Analysis. The heights are relative intensity per year, not percentages, so more 
            than one role can be high at once. Because every DBA stint also included hands-on development, the DBA band 
            lives within the broader Dev through-line, while ETL appears as intermittent spikes and then drops to zero 
            after 2021. A thin Analysis band runs across the bottom to reflect continuous—but varying—business-data work 
            (separate from DB administration, performance tuning, and development). The dashed segment after 2021 marks 
            independent project work that keeps Dev and Analysis active while DBA/ETL duties pause.</p>
